Problem

Home Deco Construction was a successful small business in Vancouver, B.C that was lacking a web platform or digital strategy.

Solution

Null Studios derived a digital strategy that included advertising on local digital publications and integrating AdWords to reach more customers online. A responsive, modern website was also developed, showcasing past projects and clients.

Result

Lead generation from online customers spiked significantly, while referrals increased significantly, as customers could simply direct their friends and family to the website, instead of referring Home Deco through word of mouth.
