About Us

The idea of Null Studios was conceived in 2014, with a mission of providing new developers with real software engineering experience, while allowing local non-profits, social enterprises, small businesses, and budding entrepreneurs launch an online presence for their company. Null Studios provides free consulting services along with affordable web development services to select organizations on a rolling application basis. Selection is based on the organization's size, mission, revenue model, website complexity/features, and community impact, with an emphasis on providing services for non-profits and social enterprises.

* Student-run organizations and initiatives automatically qualify for education pricing.

UBC Co-op Student Association of Sauder


Problem

The previous website was designed poorly, and was not very user friendly. It was poorly formatted, not SEO optimized, meaning it was always at the bottom of the list dervied by a search engine. Averaging only seven website viewers per month, the website was in dire need of a refresh.

Solution

We reviewed COSAS's existing website and content, while providing significant feedback on the user experience, look & feel, and site functionality. Understanding that most university students want a simplistic, modern interface where they can quickly find what they're looking for, we designed a one page website, integrated with an easy to access resource database for the latest resumes, cover letters, and supporting documents.

Result

The new website received tremendous support and feedback from COSAS's users, who praised the cohesive web experience and ease of use. The website had over 300 unique visitors during the first week of launch & through SEO Optimization, we were able to make the site easily searchable through search engines.
